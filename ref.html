DATA

  1. Encuesta Seguimiento a Jovenes de Hogares del Programa Oportunidades (ESJHPO).
    A Follow-up Survey conducted in 2013 focusing on the youth graduates of the Oportunidades (now Prospera) households, their education attainment, employment records and health status.
    The sample is 4,797 youths from 4,386 beneficiary households from 143 locations in 29 states of the country.
    The list is publicly available. The data set was downloaded in September 2017.

  2. Encuesta de Caracteristicas Socioeconomicas de los Hogare (ENCASEH) with updated household information.
    ENCASEH is a survey of the socio-economic conditions of rural Mexican households in the evaluation communities to determine which households would be eligible for benefits.
    The data set, which was an updated ENCASEH, was provided by Prospera. The administrative data set includes information on the household member and their gender and birth dates of the households covered in the ESJHPO survey. Only selected variables were provided to us by Prospera.
    The data set was provided by Prospera. The completed data set was provided in February 2018.

  3. Cash Transfer record of the households in ESJHPO (from 2010-2016).
    The administrative data set, provided by Prospera, includes amount of total transfer that each household receive over the period of 2010-2016. The distribution of the transfer was done every two months, and that data set include the transfer distributed in every two months. The data set shows the breakdown of the transfer (e.g. nutrition component, energy subsidies, education scholarship, etc.)
    The data set was provided by Prospera. The completed data set was provided in February 2018.
